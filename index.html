About Us

Pizza World was founded in 2010 after much demand from the general public. It started from a home kitchen in Nairobi, before friends tasted our recipes and through the word of mouth spread to all parts of the city. We are committed to providing quality for our customers and offer a wide array of choices. You can choose the type and toppings that you cherish and we will deliver them to your doorstep.
It's not a pizza if it's not from Pizza World ;)
